The Nutrition

Nutrition is an essential part to achieving your fitness goals. One must ensure they are getting the correct micronutrients and macronutrients. Macronutrients are divided into 3 categories:

  • Protein
  • Carbohydrates
  • Fat

Carbohydrates Oats, bananas, rice, potatoes, oranges, blueberries, beans.
Fats Avocado, fish, nuts, cheese, milk, eggs, yoghurt.
