Health & Fitness


One of my favourite activities to do is exercise. The essential aspects of exercising are the training and the nutrition (especially nutrition!). There are several ways in which you can plan a training routine. These can be split routine workouts or full body workouts for your weighttraining. We can go into detail about how you can plan for a weighttraining program, but we will simply discuss the essential exercises you should include in your workout. Below we discuss the essential weightlifting exercises. In addition, we discuss nutrition here, and we also include cardio workouts here. The Weights

Bench Press

The bench press is every gym bro's favourite. This is an essential exercise to get those chiseled pecks you've always dreamed of.


Liked

Back Row

The back row is key to getting a thick, strong, v-shaped back. This movement will target your rhomboids and your lats, and it can be performed in several ways, such as a barbell, dumbbells, cables pullies or a rowing machine.
